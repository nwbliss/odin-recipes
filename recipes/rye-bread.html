Rye Bread

Description

A great accompaniment for your pickle soup

Ingredients

  • 2 packages dry yeast
  • 2 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2/3 cup molasses
  • 2 tablespoons caraway seeds
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups rye flour
  • 5 cups bread flour

Steps

  1. Dissolve the yeast
  2. Make the dough
  3. Knead the dough
  4. Let the dough rise
  5. Divide the dough
  6. Shape the loaves
  7. Let the loaves rise
  8. Heat the oven
  9. Bake the loaves
  10. Eat the loaves and the fishes
